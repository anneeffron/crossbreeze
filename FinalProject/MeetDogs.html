Laki

Laki is an energetic, confident dog. He is fearless when it comes to rounding up horses and Icelandic sheep. He also loves to lay around the house and snuggle with his family as well as wrestle with his human "brother." Laki weighs 37 pounds and stands slightly over 18 inches at the shoulder. Laki is approved by the Icelandic Sheepdog Association of America for use as a stud dog. His hips have been checked by the PennHip method and found to be healthy: CHIC results. His eyes are checked yearly and registered by CERF. Here is a link to the Icelandic Sheepdog Association Web site: ISSAA. Laki is also registered with the UKC.

Vera

Vera has grown into a beautiful girl. She is 16 inches tall at the shoulder and weighs 27 pounds. Vera's OFA hip results are, "good" and her OFA elbows show no signs of dysplasia. Her PennHip results were also good. Her eyes have been checked per CERF and found to be healthy. They are rechecked yearly. Vera is sweet, obedient, and listens well to directions. She helps on the farm with sheep and horses. She absolutely LOVES to swim, anywhere, anytime!

Enja

Enja is one of our grown puppies who has come back to us. She is the daughter of Laki and Vera. Enja has had her health testing and has healthy hips and eyes. She is a UKC (United Kennel Club) Grand Champion. She has earned her Canine Good Citizen (CGC) title and has been Instinct Tested for Herding. Watch for a future litter with this sweet girl and some lucky dog!
